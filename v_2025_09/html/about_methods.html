Field and Laboratory Methods

RTD staff access sites by foot from a shoreline, bridge, or dock and collect a surface water grab sample in a 250 mL amber glass bottle using a telescoping sample pole. Visible signs of a potential HAB will be targeted if present.

Bend Genetics
Cyanotoxin samples are analyzed by Bend Genetics using an Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay or ELISA (Gold Standard Diagnostics). Cyanotoxins are extracted using the freeze-thaw method and reported as total toxin.

UC Santa Cruz
Cyanotoxin samples were analyzed by the Kudela Laboratory at University of California, Santa Cruz using liquid chromatography-mass spectrometry (LC/MS).

Quality control information is available upon request.
