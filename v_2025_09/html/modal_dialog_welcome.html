Welcome to the Delta Community HAB Dashboard, an interactive tool for exploring and downloading microcystin data from the Delta near Stockton. This data is collected through a HAB Partner Monitoring Program partnership between the Water Boards and Restore the Delta.

Disclaimer

Monitoring results provided here are considered DRAFT. Draft data are subject to change during the final review process. For more information on data review procedures, please refer to the Surface Water Ambient Monitoring Program Quality Assurance Program manual.
